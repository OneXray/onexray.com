The home page has three tabs: Outbound, Full Config, and Raw Config.

Please note: All configurations will not take effect immediately after modification. They will take effect the next time you start the VPN.

## Too long to read

For users who are not familiar with Xray-core, or users who have purchased nodes, just use the "Outbound" tab.
Set the Xray settings in the Outbounde page to "Simple", which means "simple configuration". They include a set of default diversion configurations that can meet your daily needs.

Note: You can adjust the default direct connection rules in Xray Settings according to your country or region.
